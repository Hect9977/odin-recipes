Fluffy French Toast

Breakfast is the most important meal of the day! What other way to start your day with some fluffy french toast!

This is the best French toast recipe. It's different because it uses flour. I have given it to some friends and they've all liked it better than the French toast they usually make!

Fun Fact:Wednesday is Nov. 28, and also French Toast Day on the calendar. French toast is also known as “German toast,” “eggy bread,” “gypsy toast” and “Poor Knights of Windsor.” Bread and eggs are the primary ingredients, and staler bread is preferred because it better absorbs the eggs before the mixture is fried.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 12 thick slices of bread

Directions

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in milk. Whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
  4. Working in batches, cook bread on the preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on each side. Serve hot.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving:123 calories; protein 4.8g; carbohydrates 19.4g; fat 2.7g; cholesterol 48.1mg; sodium 230.2mg.
